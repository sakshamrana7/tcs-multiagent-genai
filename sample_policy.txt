TCS CUSTOMER SUPPORT POLICIES AND GUIDELINES

================================================================================
1. RETURN POLICY
================================================================================

All items purchased from TCS are covered by our comprehensive return policy:

- 30-DAY MONEY-BACK GUARANTEE: We offer a full refund within 30 days of purchase.
- CONDITION REQUIREMENTS: Items must be unused and in original packaging.
- REFUND TIMELINE: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after receipt.
- SHIPPING COSTS: Original shipping is non-refundable unless item was defective.
- RESTOCKING FEE: Items over $500 incur a 15% restocking fee.
- DEFECTIVE ITEMS: Free return shipping provided for defective products.

To initiate a return:
1. Contact customer service with your order number
2. Receive a return shipping label
3. Ship item back in original packaging
4. Refund processed upon receipt and inspection

2. WARRANTY POLICY
================================================================================

All TCS products include comprehensive warranty coverage:

STANDARD WARRANTY (12 MONTHS):
- Covers manufacturing defects and material faults
- Covers workmanship issues
- Does NOT cover accidental damage or misuse
- Does NOT cover normal wear and tear
- Does NOT cover unauthorized repairs

EXTENDED WARRANTY OPTIONS:
- 2-Year Extended Warranty: Additional 24 months of coverage
- 3-Year Extended Warranty: Additional 36 months of coverage
- Available for purchase up to 30 days after product purchase

WARRANTY CLAIM PROCESS:
1. Report issue to support@tcs.com within 30 days of discovery
2. Provide proof of purchase and description of issue
3. Product may be inspected or replaced
4. Claims submitted after 30 days may be denied

WHAT IS NOT COVERED:
- Cosmetic damage
- Unauthorized repairs or modifications
- Damage from misuse or accidents
- Normal degradation over time
- Water damage (unless expressly covered)
